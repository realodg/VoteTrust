About VoteTrust

VoteTrust is a secure, mobile-first voting platform built to restore confidence in elections. Whether it's for classrooms, churches, associations, or national elections, VoteTrust empowers institutions to run elections that are free, fair, and trusted.

We combine biometric fingerprint authentication, real-time result tracking, and smart admin tools to eliminate fraud, reduce costs, and make voting accessible from anywhere. Our goal is simple , to make every vote count, and every voter confident.

Starting with smaller communities, we’re building trust from the ground up , proving ourselves where it matters most, and scaling up to meet the needs of larger organizations and official bodies in time.

At VoteTrust, we believe that technology shouldn’t complicate democracy ,it should protect it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about VoteTrust.

Is VoteTrust really secure?

Yes! We use biometric authentication and encrypted data storage to ensure every vote is safe and tamper-proof.

Who can use VoteTrust?

VoteTrust is built for schools, churches, clubs, and communities — and it scales up for official use in national elections too.

Does it work offline?

Voting requires internet access for real-time tracking and authentication. However, future versions may support limited offline mode.

Is my identity protected?

Absolutely. We do not store sensitive user data after verification. Your privacy is our priority.
